1. Scope of Service

Credimed Inc. ("Credimed", "we", "us") provides a document preparation and submission support service for out-of-network dental insurance reimbursement claims.

We prepare, translate, itemize, and assist in submitting your claim on your behalf based solely on the documents and information you provide.

Credimed acts solely as an administrative support service. We are not a healthcare clearinghouse, healthcare provider, insurance company, third-party administrator (TPA), licensed insurance adjuster, or billing company.

We use third-party clearinghouses to transmit prepared claims to insurance carriers. Submission methods may vary depending on insurer requirements and available systems. We do not make coverage determinations, modify clinical information, or independently verify the accuracy of submitted materials.

Claim submissions are generally targeted within twenty-four (24) hours of receiving complete documentation and your signed authorization, but submission timing is not guaranteed and may vary based on system availability, insurer requirements, or document completeness.

Credimed reserves the right to refuse service where a claim cannot be reasonably processed or where misuse of the Service is suspected.

2. Pricing, Resubmissions, and Money-Back Guarantee

2.1 Pricing (System-Determined)

Credimed uses an automated pricing engine to determine claim complexity and assign a plan:

  • Standard Plan — $49 USD
  • Plus Plan — $79 USD
  • Premium Plan — $99 USD

The assigned price is presented before payment and must be explicitly authorized by the Member.

The Member does not select the tier; pricing is determined based on claim characteristics.

Credimed does not charge any commission or percentage of your reimbursement. The plan fee, plus any optional resubmission fees, represents the full cost of the Service.

All fees are stated in U.S. dollars and are exclusive of any applicable taxes, which are the responsibility of the Member.

2.2 Resubmissions

If a claim is denied or requires correction:

  • Standard: 1 resubmission included
  • Plus: 2 resubmissions included
  • Premium: Unlimited resubmissions for 12 months

Additional resubmissions (Standard/Plus only):

  • $19 USD per attempt
  • Require explicit Member authorization
  • Capped at four (4) additional attempts per claim

All resubmissions:

  • Must relate to the same claim
  • Are subject to insurer deadlines and requirements
  • Are performed only when Credimed determines, in its reasonable judgment, that a viable correction or submission strategy exists

Additional resubmission fees are non-refundable.

2.3 Money-Back Guarantee (100% Conditional Refund)

Credimed offers a conditional refund guarantee across all pricing tiers.

We will refund 100% of the plan fee if:

  • The claim is eligible for reimbursement under your insurance policy; AND
  • The claim is not paid after all Credimed-supported resubmissions.

Eligibility Determination

For purposes of this guarantee, eligibility for reimbursement is determined based on:

  • The insurer's plan terms and coverage rules
  • The documentation provided by the Member
  • Standard coding practices

Credimed evaluates refund eligibility in good faith based on available claim data and insurer responses. Final coverage determinations remain solely the responsibility of the insurer.

Exclusions (No Refund)

The guarantee does NOT apply if denial is caused by:

  • Deductibles, coinsurance, or out-of-network limitations
  • Annual maximums, frequency limits, or waiting periods
  • Plan exclusions
  • Missing, incomplete, or inaccurate documentation
  • Fraudulent or misrepresented information
  • Failure to authorize necessary resubmissions within required deadlines

Partial Payment Rule

Any payment issued by the insurer, regardless of amount, constitutes a successful outcome. The plan fee is considered fully earned and non-refundable once any payment is made.

Final Denial

A claim is considered finally denied when:

  • All included resubmissions have been completed; AND
  • Credimed determines, in its reasonable judgment and in good faith, that no further viable submission strategy exists; OR
  • The Member declines further reasonable resubmissions.

Refund Process

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.
  • Requests must be submitted within sixty (60) days of final denial determination.

Abuse Prevention

Credimed reserves the right, acting reasonably and in good faith, to deny refunds, suspend accounts, or limit access to the Service in cases of:

  • Repeated refund requests across multiple accounts associated with the same individual or related parties
  • Coordinated use of multiple accounts or repeated submission of substantially similar claims
  • Suspected fraud, misrepresentation, or manipulation of the Service
  • Attempts to circumvent pricing, resubmission limits, or refund eligibility conditions
