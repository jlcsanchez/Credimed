Credimed Service Agreement v1.2 2026 · 1 of 1

1. What you're authorizing

You authorize Credimed Inc. to act on your behalf as a limited claims representative for the purpose of preparing and submitting one (1) dental claim to your designated US insurance carrier.

2. What we do

We translate, code (using current ADA CDT codes), format, and electronically submit your claim. We track its status and notify you of decisions.

3. What we don't do

We do not collect or hold your refund. Your insurer pays you directly using the deposit information you have on file with them.

4. Fee

You agree to pay a one-time service fee for this filing. The fee is charged when you complete checkout.

5. Money-back guarantee

If your claim is eligible and your insurer rejects it on grounds we cannot remedy through resubmission, we will refund the service fee within 5–10 business days.

6. Privacy & data

Your documents are stored encrypted at rest. We share data only with the carrier necessary to file your claim. Full privacy policy at credimed.us/legal/privacy.html.

7. Disputes

For refund eligibility disputes, email disputes@credimed.us within 30 days of the determination.
