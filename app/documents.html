What strengthens your claim

Bring everything your dentist gave you.

  • X-rays — pre and post if you have both. PDF or image.
  • Treatment plan — the itemized list with procedure codes.
  • EOB if you've already filed once and need to resubmit.
  • Before/after photos — useful for crowns, implants, and aesthetic work.

Spanish receipts are fine. We translate every line item before submitting to your US insurer — no extra step on your end.
