## 150-word description

Credimed is a healthcare-tech service that helps US dental PPO members recover out-of-network reimbursements for dental work performed by licensed providers in Mexico. The company files the insurance claim on the patient's behalf — translating Mexican procedure codes to the ADA CDT codes US insurers require, converting peso amounts to USD using the date-of-service exchange rate, and submitting the formatted claim to the insurer within 24 hours of payment.

The service is positioned for the estimated 1.4 million US dental tourists who travel to Mexico each year, half of whom pay out of pocket and never file for the partial reimbursement they're entitled to under their PPO plan. Credimed charges a one-time service fee that scales with the size of the expected refund (from $19 for low-value claims up to $99 for complex high-value ones — never more than 20% of the recovery), with a money-back guarantee — patients pay nothing if the claim is eligible and Credimed cannot recover the reimbursement.

The company is US-incorporated, HIPAA-aligned, and based in Boston, Massachusetts.

## The story

Founder Juan Luis Sanchez is a practicing dentist with several colleagues at border-area clinics in Mexico — clinics that treat US patients every week. Through his professional network, Sanchez observed firsthand how often US patients lose $1,000-$2,000 in legitimate PPO reimbursement after their dental work, not because their plans don't cover the procedures, but because the claim isn't filed in the format the insurer wants.

A typical case: a patient pays $2,600 out of pocket for crowns and a root canal in Tijuana. Files an out-of-network reimbursement claim with their PPO. Six months and four rejections later, the patient has recovered only $1,200 — and the rest is lost to a single wrong CDT code (D2750 for porcelain-fused-to-metal crowns instead of D2740 for porcelain crowns). Patients who try to fight the rejections themselves usually give up by the third or fourth round.

Roughly 1.4 million Americans travel to Mexico each year for dental procedures, drawn by prices that run 50–80% lower than US in-network rates. According to the American Dental Association, about half pay out of pocket and never file an out-of-network claim — leaving an estimated $400 million per year in eligible US PPO benefits unclaimed.

Credimed was built to capture that gap. The company files the claim correctly on the first attempt, including the CDT-code mapping, the FIX-rate currency conversion, the provider-RFC verification, and the format each insurer's claims system expects. The service fee scales with the expected refund — capped at 20% so the fee never represents an unreasonable share of what the patient recovers — and the reimbursement check goes directly from the insurer to the patient.

## What Credimed is not

- Not insurance. Credimed does not underwrite or sell insurance policies.
- Not a dental provider. Credimed does not offer medical or dental advice and does not refer patients to specific clinics.
- Not affiliated with any insurer. Credimed acts as the patient's limited claims representative under written authorization.
- Not commission-based. Credimed charges a flat fee per claim and does not take a percentage of the patient's recovered reimbursement.
