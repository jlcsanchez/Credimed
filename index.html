SERVICE AGREEMENT & PAYMENT AUTHORIZATION — Version 4.3

1. PartiesThis Agreement is between Credimed, a U.S.-incorporated company, and the Client identified during registration.
2. Scope of ServicesCredimed provides preparation, submission, and management of dental insurance reimbursement claims until full resolution.
3. Initial Setup Fee — $25 (Non-Refundable)A non-refundable $25 USD fee is due upon first claim submission regardless of outcome.
4. Success Fee — 10%Credimed earns 10% of any reimbursement. Client agrees not to change banking instructions without written consent. Fee is charged automatically after deposit verification.
5. Payment ProcessingStored securely via Stripe. Up to 3 retry attempts over 10 days on failed charges.
6. Limited Power of AttorneyClient grants Credimed a limited, irrevocable POA to contact insurer, request EOBs, and collect fees based on historical data if Client fails to report within 48 hours.
7. Non-ReportingFailure to report reimbursement within 48 hours results in immediate estimated fee charge plus 1.5% monthly interest and collection costs.
8. No ChargebackClient will not dispute the $25 fee or authorized 10% charges. All disputes must be submitted in writing to Credimed within 10 business days.
9. No GuaranteeCredimed manages claims diligently but does not guarantee insurer approval.
10. Governing LawGoverned by Delaware law. Binding arbitration. Applies to clients receiving dental care in Mexico with US insurance.
